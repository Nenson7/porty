About Us

Our Mission and Vision

At Jubilant Elite Academy, we are dedicated to fostering an environment of excellence and growth, where every student can thrive academically, socially, and personally. Our vision is to create a community of lifelong learners equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary to excel in an ever-changing world. Through innovative teaching, compassionate mentorship, and a commitment to inclusivity, we strive to empower our students to reach their highest potential. As we live by our motto, Panacea A Nostrum ("Our solution to everything"), we embrace the power of education as the ultimate remedy to the challenges and opportunities of the future.

Message from the Director

In the early days of the year 2064 B.S. I had made up my mind to make noteworthy contribution for the welfare of the nation, the society and its people. With this inspiration and from the blessing of my father and mother. I laid the foundation of Jubilant Elite Academy; an educational institution established at the heart of the town Ilam. Its official date of establishment is Bhadra 19, 2065 as registered at the office of the company Registrar, Government of Nepal, Ministry of Industry, Commerce and Supplies.

Jubilant Elite Academy is an English medium co-educational private boarding school. It's mission is to provide an excellent academic achievement for every student with an all-round outstanding development. The students are encouraged to take part in curricular activities and are even asked to join the SUPW (Socially Useful Productive Work) class. They are trained to be capable to develop a good personality. The children of kinder world are taught through Montessori Method. They have their small world confined with themselves. The entire kinder world has a very safe and fun loving environment with a beautiful garden and a wide green lawn to match the entire sloping landscape. The children here can learn while they play. Students of senior classes are taught by qualified teachers. They acquire knowledge with their involvement in the science and computer lab, and the smart class or Midas e-class helping them for grooming to excel in the competitive world.

We have a wonderful team of teachers, staffs and a managing body of the school. Talking about the school principal and teachers, "They are the best" and even beyond words can express. Most of the teachers are extremely hardworking, devoted and concerned about the students. They are capable to train their students with much care and dedication so much so that only words of application would not be able to justify their good deeds.
